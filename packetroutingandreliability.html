How data is delivered to you reliably

Information does not need to follow a fixed path. The path may change in a computer to computer conversation. The information goes from one computer to another what we called packet.

Packets does not have drivers and they does not choose any route. The packet only knows where it came from and where it is going. The packet may take different route to the same destination. Often the best route to travel is not the direct path.

We can send emails, musics, pictures etc in a packet.

How exactly do TCP connections work?

TCP allows for transmission of information in both directions. This means that computer systems that communicate over TCP can send and receive data at the same time, similar to a telephone conversation.
