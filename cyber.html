Virus:

DDOS (Distributed Denial of Service):

Hackers overwhelm a website with too many requests and we call it distributed denial when attacks come from many computers all at once Most Websites can handle millions of request but when you hit them with billions or trillions of request, they are overloaded.

PHISING

Trick people into sharing sensitive personal information. Its look like a trust worthy page or email and ask you’re to login or share your personal passwords. That is a fake page and hackers steals your information. Fortunately, there are many companies, laws and government working to make internet safer. 90% of the time when a system gets hacked is not because of a security bug but because a simple mistake made by human. Steps we all must take to avoid getting hacked 1.Use strong passwords 2.Check for authentic web addresses 3.Install system security updates often 4.Don't install software you don't trust
